Finding the Right Agent

Not all agents work the same way. The most important attribute of an agent is that he/she is well connected to the real estate industry. He/she should know the market and provide information on past sales, current listings, his or her marketing plan, and at least four solid references. In addition, you also want to look for an agent that is honest, assertive, and one that best understands your needs.
